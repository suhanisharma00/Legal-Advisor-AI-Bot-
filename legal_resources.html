Indian Legal System

Constitutional Framework

The Constitution of India is the supreme law that governs our democratic republic, ensuring justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity for all citizens.

Read Constitution

Judicial System

India's three-tier judicial system with the Supreme Court, High Courts, and subordinate courts ensures justice delivery and protection of fundamental rights.

Supreme Court

Legal Equality

The principle of equality before law ensures that justice is blind to social status, religion, caste, or economic background, providing equal treatment to all.
